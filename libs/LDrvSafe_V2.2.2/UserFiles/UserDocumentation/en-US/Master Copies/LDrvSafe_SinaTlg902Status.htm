SinaTlg902Status

Description

This function block is used to simply evaluate the Safety Integrated Functions of SINAMICS via PROFIsafe telegram 902.

Principle of operation

Structure of block LDrvSafe_SinaTlg902Status

The states of the Safety Integrated Functions of SINAMICS are represented at the output of the block.

The user must interconnect input "SinaTlg902Status" with a variable of data type "LDrvSafe_typeSinaTlg902Status".

Details on how to commission the block are provided in chapter General description.
